# Known customer-specific substitution overrides

Some customers maintain stricter compatibility requirements than the general
spec. Always consult ``score_equivalence_confidence`` for the final
customer-conditioned score before proposing a substitute.

| Customer | Restricted substitutions | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| North Atlantic Resources | `WIRE-120` (in place of `WIRE-100`) | Internal RM constraint; HT variant not yet qualified for North Sea ops. |
| Bohai Energy | `RIG-LWP-B` (in place of `RIG-LWP-A`) | Mast-height regulation in operating block prevents tall workover packages. |
| Deepwater Ventures | (no broad restrictions; case-by-case overrides) | Strict authorization tier — every substitution gets a second-line review. |

Customers not listed accept the engineering-grade substitutions per the
general spec.
